## A Journey from Understanding to Realization: The Path of Awakening  

---
![](./Images/00-不识本心-学法无益-一条从知道到体证的觉醒体验之路_images/readme.png)

### We always want to change our destiny, but we have never seen the hand that weaves our destinyour own mind.  

#### Key Highlights  
A complete and groundbreaking model of "Mind Theory" rooted in Zen wisdom and integrated with modern cognitive science:  
- One Mind: The essence of awareness, the source of life.  
- Two Mechanisms: The loss and awakening of attention and discriminating mind.  
- Three Gates: The momentary choices that determine destiny.  
- Three Networks: The elevation from the default network to the awareness network.  

#### A Clear Journey Path  
Based on this mind map, this book will guide you through six interconnected stages of awakening:  
> *Dilemma*  Seeing the True Face  *Map*  Clouds in the Sky, Water in the Bottle  *Training*  Skills Beyond Poetry  *Realm*  Neither Wind Nor Rain  *Creation*  From Autonomy to Freedom  *Completion*  The Journey Home as a Song  

#### Gratitude and Wishes  
Born in a prosperous era of China, with peace and stability, we can live in peace and turn inward to explore the fundamental wisdom of life. In this great era of cultural revival, the deep exploration of inner nature is the cornerstone of our profound cultural confidence. This journey "from understanding to realization" is an inner practice rooted in excellent traditional Chinese culture.  

May you recognize your original mind here, take back the initiative of life, live with inner peace and freedom, and return this awakening light to the great era and land we deeply love, dedicated to national rejuvenation.
